Leandro Lo

The Jiu Jitsu Champion

Black belt career

  • 2011 - CBJJ Brazilian Nationals Champion, 3rd place CBJJ Rio International Open Champion, CBJJ Rio International Open Champion
  • 2012 - IBJJF World Champion, IBJJF Pans Champion, CBJJ Brazilian Nationals Champion, CBJJ Brazilian Nationals No-Gi Champion, Copa Podio Middleweight Grand Prix Champion, 2nd place IBJJF World No-Gi Championship, 3rd place IBJJF World No-Gi Championship
  • 2013 - IBJJF World Champion, UAEJJF Abu Dhabi Pro Champion, Copa Podio Lightweight Grand Prix Champion, CBJJ Rio International Open Champion
  • 2014 - IBJJF World Champion, IBJJF Pans Champion, UAEJJF Abu Dhabi Pro Champion, Copa Podio Lightweight Grand Prix Champion, 2nd place Copa Podio Heavyweight GP, 2nd place IBJJF Pans Championship
  • 2015 - IBJJF World Champion, IBJJF Pans Champion, UAEJJF Abu Dhabi Pro Champion, 3rd place IBJJF World Championship
  • 2016 - IBJJF World Champion, IBJJF Pans Champion, UAEJJF Abu Dhabi Pro Champion, Copa Podio Lightweight Grand Prix Champion, Copa Podio Middleweight Grand Prix Champion, Copa Podio Heavyweight Grand Prix Champion, 2nd place IBJJF Pans Championship
  • 2017 -IBJJF Pans Champion, UAEJJF Abu Dhabi Pro Champion, Copa Podio Lightweight Grand Prix Champion, Copa Podio Middleweight Grand Prix Champion, Copa Podio Heavyweight Grand Prix Champion, 2nd place IBJJF Pans Championship
  • 2018 - IBJJF World Champion, IBJJF Pans Champion, 2nd place IBJJF World Championship
  • 2019 - IBJJF World Champion, 2nd place IBJJF World Championship
  • 2021 - 3rd place IBJJF World Championship
  • 2022 - IBJJF World Champion
